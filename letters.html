A Poem

“love is patient, love is kind”
this is what you taught me
and it stuck in my mind
when I hurt my sister, or fight a friend
You never shouted, but talked carefully instead

“Love is not rude, it is not self-seeking”
smiles on your faces, especially when you’re giving
you do what you do because you love to
you do what you do, even if you don't have to

“Love always protects, always trusts”
when I’m in the wrong, you do what is just
but you trust me to do what I want again
because you see the best in me, and you support me

Love never fails even if things fall apart
it will endure even through the most difficult times
Thank you for being our pillars and our bridge
I love you Mama, I love you Papa

To my mom,

Hi Mom! How are you? I know I see you everyday and sleep beside you every night, but I never get to ask how you are. I can see how busy you get with work. But mom, your health is more important that work and I know you know that. Try sleeping before 4 am :( I’m getting worried about you. Every since Papa migrated with Kuya, you stress yourself out more than usual. You try to take all the responsibilities - bringing Alex to school, waking up early to bring her to training, doing the groceries by yourself, and all that. You deserve some rest :(

It wasn’t easy, we know, because Papa used to do all that for us, but now he’s not at home anymore, you feel like you have to take his place. Well we’re here to tell you that you dont have to do that! We’re all grown up kids now who can look after ourselves. You have done more than enough. And because of that, I want to take this opportunity to thank you. Thank you for being patient. Thank you for trusting us with our ways. Thank you for being the Mom and the Dad at home. You are the strongest woman I know, and you serve as my inspiration. I love you Mom. Let us take care of you.



To Papa,
